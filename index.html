Privacy Policy – Pizza Boyz NZ – Delivery App

Last updated: 11 December 2025

Pizza Boyz NZ – Delivery App (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a food ordering and delivery platform that allows users to browse menus, place orders, and track deliveries. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information when you create an account or place an order:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Profile details
  • Order and purchase history

1.2 Location Information

We may collect approximate (coarse) location information to:

  • Show nearby restaurants
  • Determine delivery availability
  • Improve order accuracy

We do not collect precise GPS location and do not use location data for tracking.

1.3 Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through third-party payment providers (such as Stripe). We do not collect, store, or have access to your card or banking details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for app functionality, including:

  • Account creation and login
  • Order placement and delivery
  • Customer support
  • Payment processing
  • Order history and re-ordering

We do not use your data for advertising or tracking across apps or websites.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information only with:

  • Restaurants fulfilling your orders
  • Delivery partners
  • Secure payment providers

We never sell your personal information.

4. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. While we strive to safeguard your data, no system is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Permanently delete your account and associated data

Account deletion can be initiated directly within the app:
Profile → Settings → Delete Account

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated revision date.
