Privacy Policy – Pizza Boyz NZ – Delivery App

Last updated: 11 December 2025

Pizza Boyz NZ – Delivery App (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a food ordering and delivery platform that allows users to browse menus, place orders, make payments, and track deliveries. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Profile details
  • Order history

1.2 Device Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address

1.3 Location Information

  • Show restaurants near you
  • Deliver your orders accurately
  • Improve app service

1.4 Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through third-party payment providers. We do NOT store your card or banking details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation & login
  • Order placement and delivery
  • Customer support
  • Payment processing
  • Service improvement
  • Fraud prevention
  • App performance and analytics

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Delivery partners
  • Restaurants
  • Secure payment providers
  • Service providers (analytics, crash reporting)

We never sell your personal information.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Account deletion
  • Restrictions on processing

You can make these requests via the app or email.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated date will always be shown at the top.
